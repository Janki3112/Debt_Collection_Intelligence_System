# Sample Contract Sources

All sample contracts used for testing in the Contract Intelligence API are publicly available and provided for educational and non-commercial use only. Each document demonstrates different clause structures useful for testing clause extraction, question answering (RAG), and risk analysis.

---

## 1. Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (UK Government)
- **Filename**: `Example-Mutual-Non-Disclosure-Agreement.pdf`
- **Source**: GOV.UK (UK Government)
- **URL**: https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/5a8188fd40f0b62302697d5e/Example-Mutual-Non-Disclosure-Agreement.pdf
- **License**: Open Government License (UK)
- **Description**: Official UK government template for a Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement. Clearly structured and ideal for identifying confidentiality, term, and signatory clauses. Serves as a clean base document for testing entity extraction and clause segmentation.

## 2. Master Services Agreement (Mercy Corps)
- **Filename**: `sample_msa.pdf`
- **Source**: Mercy Corps (Nonprofit Organization)
- **URL**: https://www.mercycorps.org/sites/default/files/2020-01/Attachment%202%20-%20Sample%20Master%20Service%20Agreement.pdf
- **License**: Public Contract Template
- **Description**: A comprehensive Master Services Agreement (MSA) used by Mercy Corps. Contains detailed clauses on indemnity, limitation of liability, termination, and deliverables. Excellent for clause classification, risk flagging, and payment-term extraction.

## 3. Mutual Confidentiality Agreement (Tennessee Tech University)
- **Filename**: `NDA_Mutual_template2_5_2019.pdf`
- **Source**: Tennessee Tech University
- **URL**: https://www.tntech.edu/research/pdf/researchcompliance/NDA_Mutual_template2_5_2019.pdf
- **License**: Public University Template (Educational Use)
- **Description**: University-issued Mutual Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement. Includes signature blocks, term length, and information protection scope. Useful for evaluating metadata extraction (effective date, parties, signatures).

## 4. Master Services Agreement (GlobalSign)
- **Filename**: `GlobalSign_Master_Services_Agreement.pdf`
- **Source**: GlobalSign (Corporate Repository)
- **URL**: https://www.globalsign.com/en/repository/GlobalSign_Master_Services_Agreement.pdf
- **License**: Publicly Posted Corporate Legal Document
- **Description**: Real-world corporate MSA outlining service scope, liability caps, governing law, and termination rights. Useful for training or benchmarking payment-related clause extraction and corporate compliance detection.

## 5. Sample Terms of Service (iubenda)
- **Filename**: `Sample-Terms-of-Service-Template_PDF-3.pdf`
- **Source**: iubenda (Privacy & Compliance Platform)
- **URL**: https://www.iubenda.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Sample-Terms-of-Service-Template_PDF-3.pdf
- **License**: CC-BY 4.0 (Creative Commons Attribution)
- **Description**: A clean, modern Terms of Service example for a generic website. Covers key sections such as governing law, arbitration, and limitation of liability. Ideal for testing ToS parsing and clause retrieval models.
